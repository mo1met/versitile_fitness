Josh Williams, C.P.T & C.N.C

Founder and Coach

I have been a fitness enthusiast for the majority of my life. My passion for health and fitness led me to make a transition from a successful career in business to a career in personal training. With this experience, I realized I could expand the number of people I could help by creating an online fitness training and nutrition coaching platform. I formed Versatile Fitness & Nutrition to fulfill that ambition. I specialize in weight loss and weight gain (the right way), nutrition, bodybuilding, and strength training for clients of all ages and fitness levels. When I’m not training or working on my own fitness, I enjoy causing trouble with my kitten and relaxing with a video game or bingeing an entire Netflix series on a day off.
